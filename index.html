Moment Catch

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Moment Catch handles information when you use the app. Moment Catch is designed to record video, flag key moments, save clips, and let you review or share those clips from your device.

Effective date: April 12, 2026

Information We Access

Moment Catch may request access to the following device features:

  • Camera access to record video.
  • Microphone access to record audio with your videos.
  • Photo library access when you choose to save clips to your device.

How Your Data Is Used

Moment Catch uses this access only to provide the core app functionality:

  • Recording videos inside the app.
  • Creating flagged clips from recorded videos.
  • Saving videos or clips locally on your device.
  • Sharing videos or clips only when you choose to do so.

Storage

Videos, flagged clips, thumbnails, and related metadata are stored locally on your device. Moment Catch does not require an account to use these core features.

Sharing

If you use the share features, the selected video or clip is shared through the system share sheet on your device. Moment Catch only initiates sharing when you choose that action.

Analytics and Tracking

Moment Catch does not sell your personal information. Based on the current app functionality, the app is not designed to track you across third-party apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

Moment Catch is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe information has been provided in error, please contact the publisher using the support information provided in the app listing.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
